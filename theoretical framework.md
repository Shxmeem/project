# **A Study on Factors Influencing People's Preference for UPI, Card or Cash for Daily Transactions**

## **Chapter 3: Theoretical Framework**

### **3.1** Definition of Payment Systems

A payment system refers to the methods used for transferring money in exchange for goods and services. The major payment methods used in India and within Malappuram Municipality today are **UPI**, **debit/credit cards**, and **cash**.

The evolution of payment systems has improved speed, convenience, and accessibility. The choice of payment method varies based on user preference and situation. Payment systems play an important role in everyday economic activities of people in Malappuram Municipality.

### **3.2** Definition of UPI (Unified Payments Interface)

**Unified Payments Interface (UPI)** is a real-time digital payment system developed by NPCI in 2016. It allows instant fund transfer between bank accounts through mobile apps. In Malappuram Municipality, UPI is widely used for small purchases, bill payments, and peer-to-peer transfers.

UPI supports merchant payments, bill payments, and online transactions. It works 24/7 and does not require sharing bank details. UPI has become one of the most popular digital payment systems in the study area.

### **3.3** Features of Payment Methods

**Key Features:**

*   Instant money transfer through **UPI** in daily transactions.
*   24/7 availability of digital payment services.
*   QR code-based payments for easy merchant use in shops and markets.
*   **Card payments** support online and high-value purchases.
*   **Cash** provides universal acceptance without the need for technology.

### **3.4** Characteristics of Payment Systems

**Main Characteristics:**

*   Convenience in completing transactions quickly.
*   Speed of payment processing.
*   Security and privacy in transactions.
*   Accessibility across different population groups.
*   Reliability for regular financial activities in Malappuram Municipality.

### **3.5** Importance of Payment Systems

**Significance:**

*   Promotes financial inclusion among the local population.
*   Reduces dependency on physical **cash** in daily transactions.
*   Supports the growth of small businesses and online services.
*   Improves transparency in financial transactions.
*   Encourages the development of a digital economy in Malappuram Municipality.

### **3.6** Challenges of Payment Methods

**Key Challenges:**

*   Fear of online fraud and cybercrime among some users.
*   Network and internet connectivity issues in certain areas.
*   Lack of digital awareness among elderly users.
*   Technical failures such as transaction decline or delay.
*   Resistance to change due to strong habit of using **cash**.

### **3.7** Limitations of Payment Methods

**Major Limitations:**

*   **UPI** requires smartphone and internet access.
*   **Card payments** depend on POS machine availability.
*   **Cash** can be lost, stolen, or damaged.
*   Digital payments may involve service charges in some cases.
*   Limited acceptance of digital payments in some small shops.
